Sudoku Solver

Rules

The goal of the game is to fill in the board such that each row, column, and 3x3 sub-grid has the numbers 1-9 with no duplicates.

Test your skills and see how fast you can solve this puzzle

Notes

You can only check your solution after all cells have been filled.

When you check your work, if your solution is correct, the timer will stop. otherwise, you'll be told if you have mistakes.

All puzzles have a unique solution.
